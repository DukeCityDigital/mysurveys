Consent form

Purpose of the study: This study investigates parents’ beliefs about different issues, including vaccination and GM food. This study is for groups of friends. To qualify for the study, you and two of your friends will need to sign up on this website. The friends will have to be from different households and live in the U.S.

A week or two after you and your two friends have signed up, you will be invited to participate in three study waves, and your friends in one study wave. Study waves will be a few weeks apart and take no more than 10 minutes each. You will be asked questions such as “Is growing GM crops harmful or beneficial to the environment?”, “Are childhood vaccines natural or unnatural?”, and “Do you have children under 18 years of age at home?”.

Payment: After completing the first study wave, you will receive $10 for each friend that has signed up, and $5 for completing the study (for a total of $25). For each next study wave you participate in, you will receive an additional $5 (for up to additional $10). Each of your friends will get $10 for signing up and $5 for completing the first wave.

Importantly, if none of your friends sign up, we won’t able to invite you to the study or compensate you. If only one of your friends signs up, we again won’t be able to invite you to the study or compensate you, but we will still compensate your friend for signing up.

Benefits and Risks: This project will generate useful and interesting findings on how people make up their minds and influence each other.There are no known risks in this research, but some individuals may experience discomfort or perceive loss of privacy when answering questions.

Your privacy: MySurveys collects your data so we can select and contact participants for studies and analyze the anonymized results. There are no names or identifying information associated with your responses. Data will be kept securely and not shared with anyone else. Your personal information will only be used for compensation and will not be connected to your answers. This website also uses cookies to ensure you get the best experience. You can find out more about our privacy policy by clicking here. By continuing to use this website, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to our full privacy terms.

Right to withdraw from the study: Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You have the right to choose not to participate or to withdraw your participation at any point in this study without penalty.

Contact information: This study is conducted by Mirta Galesic and Henrik Olsson from the Santa Fe Institute, funded by the NSF. If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about the research, please feel free to call the researchers at (505) 946-2703 or email at support@mysurveys.santafe.edu. If you have questions regarding your rights as a research participant, or about what you should do in case of any harm to you, or if you want to obtain information or offer input, please contact the UNM Office of the IRB (OIRB) at (505) 277-2644 or irb@unm.edu.
