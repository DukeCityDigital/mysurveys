You can open a PayPal account at www.paypal.com in just a few minutes.

You will only need to provide your name, email, and mailing address (SciFriends will not have access to this information). Your PayPal email address can be the same as the one you used to sign up for SciFriends, but you can also use another one.

In order to spend or withdraw your SciFriends payments, you will have to verify your PayPal account by adding a credit card or bank account to your PayPal Account.

For more detailed instructions on opening a PayPal account, click here

Once you have a PayPal account, getting a PayPal.Me link is easy. In your PayPal account, click on “Send & Request” menu in the top bar, and then select “Get your PayPal.Me link” in the bottom right part of the screen.

On the next page, select the link you wish to use and click on “Grab this link”. Finally, on the next screen, you will have the opportunity to select whether you will use the link for Friends & family or Goods & services. You can select either, but you can start with Friends & family. You will also be asked to add a photo – this is not necessary. Click Create Your PayPal.Me when you’re ready. Then Copy the link and paste it in the form below, along with your Primary PayPal email address.
